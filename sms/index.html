1. How SMS Consent Is Obtained

Arizona AI Solutions sends SMS messages only in response to a customer's inbound phone call. We never send cold outreach, unsolicited, promotional, or marketing texts.

During each inbound call, the AI agent asks the customer a clear consent question:

Example consent prompt: "Would you like me to send your booking confirmation via text message?"

The customer must verbally confirm (e.g., "Yes," "Sure," "Go ahead") before any SMS is sent. If the customer declines or does not respond, no text message is sent and the confirmation is provided verbally on the call or via email if an email address was provided.

Consent is strictly limited to transactional messages related to that specific service interaction. Consent does not extend to promotional offers, marketing campaigns, newsletters, seasonal discounts, or any messages unrelated to that appointment. Consent never authorizes marketing or promotional messages under any circumstances.

Each new inbound call requires fresh verbal consent before any new SMS messages are sent. Prior consent from a previous call does not carry over.

2. Opt-In Methods

End users opt in to receive SMS messages via the following method(s):

  • Verbal consent during an inbound call — Customer calls a business number managed by Arizona AI Solutions and verbally consents to receiving SMS confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups.
  • UNSTOP keyword — If a customer previously opted out via STOP, they may send UNSTOP to the originating number to restore message delivery. Opt-in consent is reaffirmed at this point.

We do not offer web-based opt-in, keyword-based cold opt-in, or any opt-in method that does not originate from an inbound call.

3. Message Types

We send the following message types, and only these:

  • Booking confirmations — Sent immediately after an appointment is booked on the call
  • Appointment reminders — Sent approximately 24 hours before a scheduled appointment
  • Reschedule confirmations — Sent when a customer requests to change an appointment time
  • Post-appointment follow-ups — Sent approximately 1 hour after a completed appointment, including a review request link
  • Emergency notifications — Sent to the caller confirming that their emergency request has been received and a technician is being dispatched

We do not send: promotional offers, marketing campaigns, seasonal discounts, newsletters, or any messages unrelated to the customer's specific service interaction.

4. Message Frequency

Customers can expect 1 to 3 SMS messages per appointment:

  • Message 1: Booking confirmation — sent immediately upon booking
  • Message 2: Appointment reminder — sent approximately 24 hours before the appointment
  • Message 3: Post-appointment follow-up with review link — sent approximately 1 hour after the appointment

This is a transactional, event-triggered message pattern. There is no recurring or subscription-based messaging. If a customer reschedules, an additional reschedule confirmation message may be sent.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

Customers may opt out of SMS messages at any time using the following keywords:

  • STOP — Immediately cancels all future SMS messages. After sending STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message stating that you have been unsubscribed. No further messages will be sent unless you re-opt in.
  • UNSTOP — Restores SMS message delivery. You will receive a confirmation message that messaging has been resumed. Opt-in consent is reaffirmed at this point.
  • HELP — Replies with a help message containing support contact information and a reminder that you can reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Opt-out requests are processed immediately upon receipt. There is no delay between receiving a STOP command and halting message delivery.

6. Data & Privacy

Arizona AI Solutions collects only the minimum information needed to provide the service: phone number, caller name (provided verbally), email (via Calendly booking), and service details. We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or financial data through our phone or SMS systems.

All data is stored on the client's dedicated private server and is not shared with other clients or third parties. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

7. Carrier Compliance

  • Arizona AI Solutions operates under a registered A2P 10DLC brand and campaign with The Campaign Registry (TCR)
  • All SMS messaging complies with Twilio's Acceptable Use Policy, A2P 10DLC requirements, and CTIA Messaging Principles
  • Message content is limited to transactional, event-driven communications as described above
  • Arizona AI Solutions monitors delivery rates, opt-out rates, and spam reports and will suspend or modify messaging practices if carrier thresholds are approached
