SMS Messaging Disclosure

By calling a business phone number managed by Arizona AI Solutions, you may receive SMS text messages. These messages are transactional only and are sent only after you provide verbal consent during the call.

Message types:

  • Booking confirmations — sent after an appointment is booked
  • Appointment reminders — sent approximately 24 hours before your appointment
  • Reschedule confirmations — sent if you request a schedule change
  • Post-appointment follow-ups — sent after your service is complete

Frequency: 1–3 messages per appointment. No recurring or subscription messages.

No marketing: We never send promotional offers, ads, or newsletters.

Opt out anytime: Reply STOP to cancel all messages. Reply HELP for support. Reply UNSTOP to restore delivery.
