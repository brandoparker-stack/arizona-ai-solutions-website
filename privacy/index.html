Information We Collect

When a customer calls a business phone number managed by Arizona AI Solutions, we may collect:

  • Phone number — to send booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and follow-up messages
  • Caller name — provided during the call for appointment booking
  • Email address — only if the customer chooses to receive confirmation via email
  • Service details — type of service requested, appointment time, and business name
  • Call metadata — call duration, time of call, and call outcome (booked, missed, emergency)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or financial data.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Answer inbound phone calls on behalf of the business
  • Send booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and reschedule links via SMS
  • Send post-appointment follow-up messages and review request links via SMS
  • Notify the business owner of emergency calls
  • Log call activity for the business via the dashboard

We do not use collected information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the call.

SMS Messaging Practices

  • Opt-in: Customers opt in by calling the business phone number. During the call, the AI agent asks if the customer would like to receive their booking confirmation or appointment details via text. The customer verbally confirms before any SMS is sent.
  • Message types: Booking confirmations, appointment reminders, reschedule links, post-appointment follow-ups, review request links, and emergency notifications.
  • Frequency: Typically 1–3 messages per appointment (confirmation, reminder, follow-up). No recurring or subscription-based messaging.
  • Opt-out: Customers can reply STOP at any time to cancel all future SMS messages. Reply UNSTOP to resume. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Storage and Security

  • Each client business runs on a private virtual server managed by Arizona AI Solutions
  • Customer data is stored on the client's dedicated server and is not shared between clients
  • Data is not used to train AI models or machine learning systems
  • Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising
  • Access to call logs and dashboard is restricted to the business owner and Arizona AI Solutions administrators

Data Retention

  • Call logs and SMS records are retained for the duration of the client's service agreement
  • Upon termination, client data is deleted from the server within 30 days
  • Backups containing client data are purged within 90 days of termination

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to deliver our phone and SMS functionality:

  • Twilio — call routing, SMS delivery, and phone number provisioning
  • Calendly — appointment scheduling and booking confirmation
  • xAI (Grok) — AI voice agent for answering calls

Each service provider operates under their own privacy policy and data processing agreements.

Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com.
