Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2026
Business: Arizona AI Solutions
Contact: brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com

Information We Collect

When a customer calls a business phone number managed by Arizona AI Solutions, we may collect:

  • Phone number — to identify the caller, send booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and follow-up messages via SMS
  • Caller name — provided verbally during the call for appointment booking and personalization
  • Email address — collected through the scheduling integration (Calendly) when a customer books an appointment, used for appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Service details — type of service requested, preferred appointment time, and the business name the customer called
  • Call metadata — call duration, time of call, call outcome (booked, missed, emergency), and call transcript (a text-based summary of the conversation, not an audio recording)

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or financial data through our phone or SMS systems. We do not record or store call audio — only text-based transcripts are retained.

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Answer inbound phone calls on behalf of the business using an AI voice agent
  • Send booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and reschedule links via SMS
  • Send post-appointment follow-up messages and review request links via SMS
  • Notify the business owner of emergency calls
  • Log call activity for the business via the Arizona AI Call Dashboard

We do not use collected information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the customer's inbound call and service request.

SMS Messaging Practices

How SMS Consent Is Obtained

All SMS messages sent through Arizona AI Solutions are triggered only by the customer's inbound phone call. We never send cold outreach, promotional, or marketing texts.

During an inbound call, the AI agent asks the customer a clear consent question such as:

"Would you like me to send your booking confirmation via text message?"

The customer must verbally confirm (e.g., "Yes" or "Sure") before any SMS is sent. If the customer declines, no text message is sent and the confirmation is provided verbally on the call or via email if the customer provides an email address.

Consent is strictly limited to transactional messages only — appointment confirmations, reminders, rescheduling, and follow-ups related to that specific service interaction. Consent does not extend to promotional offers, marketing campaigns, newsletters, seasonal discounts, or any messages unrelated to that appointment. Consent never authorizes marketing or promotional messages under any circumstances. Each new inbound call requires fresh verbal consent before any new SMS messages are sent.

Message Types

We send the following message types, and only these:

  • Booking confirmations — sent immediately after an appointment is booked on the call
  • Appointment reminders — sent approximately 24 hours before a scheduled appointment
  • Reschedule links — sent when a customer requests to change an appointment time
  • Post-appointment follow-ups — sent approximately 1 hour after a completed appointment
  • Review request links — included in post-appointment follow-up messages
  • Emergency notifications — sent to the business owner when a caller reports an emergency (burst pipe, gas leak, etc.)

We do not send: promotional offers, marketing campaigns, seasonal discounts, newsletters, or any messages unrelated to the customer's specific service interaction.

Message Frequency

Customers can expect 1 to 3 SMS messages per appointment:

  • Message 1: Booking confirmation — sent immediately upon booking
  • Message 2: Appointment reminder — sent approximately 24 hours before the appointment
  • Message 3: Post-appointment follow-up with review link — sent approximately 1 hour after the appointment

This is a transactional, event-triggered message pattern. There is no recurring or subscription-based messaging. If a customer reschedules, an additional reschedule confirmation message may be sent.

Opt-Out Instructions

Customers may opt out of SMS messages at any time using the following keywords:

  • STOP — Immediately cancels all future SMS messages from our service. After sending STOP, you will receive a single confirmation message stating that you have been unsubscribed. No further messages will be sent unless you re-opt in by calling the business again and providing verbal consent, or by sending UNSTOP.
  • UNSTOP — Restores SMS message delivery. You will receive a confirmation message that messaging has been resumed. Opt-in consent is reaffirmed at this point.
  • HELP — Replies with a help message containing support contact information and a reminder that you can reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Opt-out requests are processed immediately upon receipt. There is no delay — once a STOP command is received and confirmed, no further messages will be sent to that phone number. If you believe you are receiving messages in error after opting out, please contact us at brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com.

Data Storage and Security

  • Each client business runs on dedicated infrastructure we control — client data is logically and physically separated, never shared with other clients
  • Customer data is stored on the client's dedicated instance and is never shared with other clients
  • Data is not used by Arizona AI Solutions to train models — we do not use your call data, transcripts, or customer information to train our own or third-party models
  • Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond service delivery
  • Access to call logs and the Arizona AI Call Dashboard is restricted to the business owner and authorized Arizona AI Solutions administrators
  • All data transmissions between our services and mobile carriers are encrypted using industry-standard TLS
  • Servers are hosted in secure data centers with access controls and monitoring

Third-Party Services

To deliver our phone and SMS functionality, we share limited data with the following service providers. Each provider is contractually obligated to process data only as necessary for the services they provide, and each operates under their own privacy policy:

  • Twilio Inc. — Receives: customer phone numbers, SMS message content (confirmations, reminders, follow-ups), message delivery status, and call routing metadata. Purpose: call routing, SMS message delivery, and phone number provisioning. Twilio processes this data as a communications carrier under their Acceptable Use Policy and A2P 10DLC guidelines. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Calendly — Receives: customer name, phone number, email (if provided), and appointment details. Purpose: appointment scheduling, availability checking, and booking confirmation generation. Calendly Privacy Policy
  • xAI (Grok) — Receives: real-time voice audio stream during the call for AI processing. Purpose: powering the AI voice agent that answers calls and interacts with customers. xAI does not retain call audio after processing. xAI Privacy Policy

Each provider operates under their own privacy policy and data processing agreements. We do not share customer data with any other third parties, data brokers, or advertising networks.

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Call logs, SMS records, and booking data are retained for the duration of the client's active service agreement
  • Upon account termination: Client data (call logs, SMS records, booking data, customer information) is deleted from the client's dedicated instance within 30 calendar days
  • Backup retention: Encrypted backups containing client data are purged within 90 calendar days of termination
  • SMS delivery records: Message delivery receipts and opt-out logs are retained for 12 months for regulatory compliance (A2P 10DLC carrier requirements), then automatically deleted
  • Data export: Before deletion, the business owner may request a complete export of all data in their account by contacting brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com

Your Rights

  • Access: You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you by contacting brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request, except where retention is required by law
  • Opt-out of SMS: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from our service. Opt-out is processed immediately
  • Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our service is directed to adult consumers seeking home service businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a call or SMS interaction involves a child under 13, we will delete that data promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to active business clients via email at least 30 days before taking effect. The latest version will always be available at arizona-ai-solutions.com/privacy. For customers who interact with our SMS service, continued receipt of messages after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
