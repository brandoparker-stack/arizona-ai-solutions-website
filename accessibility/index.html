Accessibility Statement

Arizona AI Solutions is committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet WCAG 2.2 Level AA standards across all pages.

What We've Done

  • Semantic HTML: Proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3), landmarks (<main>, <nav>, <footer>), and list structures throughout.
  • Keyboard navigation: All interactive elements are reachable via keyboard. Skip-to-content link, visible focus indicators, and logical tab order.
  • Screen reader support: ARIA labels on form inputs, aria-live regions for dynamic content (calculator results), and hidden decorative elements.
  • Form accessibility: All form fields have explicit <label> elements. Required fields are marked. Error states use aria-invalid.
  • Color contrast: All text meets WCAG AA minimum contrast ratios (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text and UI components).
  • Responsive design: Pages remain usable at 200% zoom and on mobile devices.
  • No auto-playing media: No content auto-plays or flashes.
  • Descriptive links: All links describe their destination (no "click here" links).

Known Limitations

  • Our calculator tool uses dynamic JavaScript for results. While we've added aria-live regions, some screen readers may need a moment to announce updated values.
  • Embedded third-party content (Calendly widget, if any) may have its own accessibility limitations outside our control.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on our site, please let us know. We take accessibility seriously and will respond promptly.

  • Email: brandon@arizona-ai-solutions.com

Standards

This site targets conformance with WCAG 2.2 Level AA. We periodically audit our pages and remediate issues as they're found.
